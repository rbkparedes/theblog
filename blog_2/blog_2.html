K-Dogs

You Hungry?

It is actually insane how many food stands there are in Camden Market, but most of them kind of sell the same thing which is the English version of Chinese food. It can be pretty overwhelming as they are shouting at you to try to get you to try their food in hopes that you will buy some. In a quiet, slightly hidden alley is where I found this interesting looking Korean corn dog place. I had been wanting to try a Korean corn dog for a while, so this was the perfect time to do so. I really recommend this place if you want something quick and easy to eat while you browse around the market.
